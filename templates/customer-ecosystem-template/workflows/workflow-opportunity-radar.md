# Workflow Opportunity Radar

Produce 3-5 workflow opportunities before private integration.

Each opportunity must include:

- workflow name;
- customer problem hypothesis;
- public basis;
- required private sources;
- confidence;
- risk lane;
- metric candidates;
- customer confirmation question;
- near-term next action.

Do not include exact savings, ROI, or productivity claims until customer-approved internal data validates the baseline.
